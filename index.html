Dear Traders,

It is with sincere regret that I inform you of the decision to close Down2Trade at the end of 2024. This decision has been one of the hardest I've had to make, and it comes after considerable reflection on our values and the sustainability of our business.

From the beginning, we committed to a standard of honesty, transparency, and integrity. We chose to prioritize ethical practices over higher profits, focusing steadfastly on providing genuine support to help our clients succeed in trading.

Despite the success of this approach in building trust and a loyal community, it has become apparent that maintaining our trading rooms is no longer economically viable.

We are proud to conclude this chapter knowing we never compromised on our mission. Our dedication to maintaining an honest and client-focused environment remains intact, though it means accepting the financial reality that our trading rooms can no longer sustain themselves.

I am profoundly grateful for your support and engagement with Down2Trade. It has been a privilege to serve such a passionate community of traders. While this phase of our journey ends, the lessons and values we've shared will continue to inspire and guide us all. Thank you for being a part of our story, and I wish you success and fulfillment in all your future trading endeavors.

Warm regards,

Ahmet Candan
Founder, Down2Trade
